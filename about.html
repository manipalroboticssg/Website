About MRSG

Manipal Robotics Support Group (or MRSG for short) is the largest community of robotics enthusiasts in Manipal, comprising students, alumni and professors working in the field of robotics and automation and its related fields. Our main aim is to provide a welcoming space to learn new skills and collaborate with other community members on projects, encompassing a vast demographic ranging from complete beginners to seasoned veterans.

MRSG started in June 2021, when a group of students and alumni from Manipal Institute of Technology, Manipal, got together to grow a community of like-minded robotics enthusiasts to create a platform with low barriers of entry for anyone seeking to dip their toes into the ever-changing field of robotics and automation, after seeing new heights of interest in robotic process applications during the COVID-19 pandemic. The primary focus was on increasing accessibility of knowledge pertaining the to more esoteric topics of the focal trinity of Artificial Intelligence, Electronics and Control Systems, and Design and Modelling, while also endeavouring to introduce more students to the possibilities of the sector.

Apart from maintaining an active Discord server for members to interact with each other, we also hold regular workshops and events collaborating with many renowned technical clubs in Manipal. We also maintain a blog for members to publish technical articles, giving them an opportunity to sharpen their research and writing skills.
